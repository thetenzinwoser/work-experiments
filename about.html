Our Story

Born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a moment of peace.

Stillness began in 2022 when our founders — a meditation teacher and a software designer — noticed something: the people who needed mindfulness most felt it was out of reach. Too mystical. Too time-consuming. Too intimidating.

So they built something different. A space that meets you where you are, whether that's five minutes on a lunch break or a deep evening practice. No jargon. No pressure. Just gentle guidance toward a calmer mind.

Today, Stillness serves over 50,000 people across 40 countries. Our library of 200+ meditations is crafted by experienced teachers who understand that peace isn't a destination — it's a practice.

What Guides Us

Simplicity

Meditation should feel effortless, not like another task on your list.

Inclusivity

Mindfulness belongs to everyone, regardless of background or experience.

Presence

We practice what we teach — building with intention and mindful design.

Growth

Every session is a step. We celebrate the journey, not just the milestone.

Our Philosophy

We believe technology should bring you closer to yourself, not further away. Every feature we build, every meditation we record, every word we write is in service of one thing: helping you find a few moments of genuine stillness in a noisy world.
